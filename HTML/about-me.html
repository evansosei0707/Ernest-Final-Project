About Ernesto Shoes

Ernesto Shoes was started by Ernest Sarfo Boateng, The venture started producing shoes only this year. In addition to female and male, there is a children's line. Shoes of non-dull styles and rich colors immediately catch the eye.

We are located in Accra, Ghana along Nkrumah 24th Avenue.
Being an official Nike partner, we sell their shoes at convenient prices. We focus on team-designed collaborations that appeal to our global community of global independents, creatives, and free-thinkers. We create shoes to reflect the unbound lives we live and don't stop until we get it right.
